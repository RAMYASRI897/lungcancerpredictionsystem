Symptoms Let's Predict Suggestions

Asthama

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition that causes inflammation and narrowing of the airways, leading to difficulty breathing, wheezing, and shortness of breath.

COPD

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) is a progressive lung disease that causes airflow limitation, leading to breathing difficulties, coughing, and increased mucus production.

SHORTNESS OF BREATH

Shortness of breath, or dyspnea, is a condition where an individual experiences difficulty or discomfort in breathing, often due to underlying health issues like lung or heart disease.

cystic-fibrosis

Cystic fibrosis is a genetic disorder that causes severe damage to the lungs, digestive system, and other organs, leading to persistent respiratory infections and difficulty breathing.

TUBERCULOSIS

Tuberculosis (TB) is a contagious bacterial infection that primarily affects the lungs, causing coughing, chest pain, and fatigue.

PNEUMONIA

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs, leading to symptoms like cough, fever, and difficulty breathing.
